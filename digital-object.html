Alexander's Ragtime Band (1911)

"Alexander's Ragtime Band" is a popular ragtime composition written by Irving Berlin. The 1911 recording featured in the National Jukebox collection represents the early era of recorded sound and the rise of commercial music distribution.

This recording reflects how music was distributed before streaming platforms existed. Listeners purchased physical records and relied on phonographs for playback.
